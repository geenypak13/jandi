FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Honest answers — never inflated, never lowballed.

How much does a custom app or website cost?＋
There’s no fixed price list, because it depends on the scope of what we build. I see your site first, then give an honest quote — never inflated, never lowballed.
You’re a one-person company — can I really trust you with this?＋
Yes. I’ve built and run things like attendance apps and payment-integrated websites myself. Being solo actually means one person takes responsibility and communicates with you directly, from start to finish.
What does “the app becomes mine” mean? (BYOC)＋
It means the app, the data, and the accounts are all created in your name. I only build and maintain them — I never hold onto your information. You can take all of your data with you at any time.
Is it an app that goes on the App Store?＋
Most are “web apps (PWA).” Add it to your phone’s home screen and it works just like an app. No App Store review or fees — you can start fast and light.
How long does it take to build?＋
It depends on the scope, but a simple tool can start quickly. We set the schedule together after I’ve seen your site.
What industries do you work with?＋
Restaurants, cafés, catering, care services — no limits. If there’s an annoying task you repeat every day, I can usually help.
What if I need changes later, or something breaks?＋
I fix it myself. I built it, so nothing goes through a middleman. Unlike outsourcing, the line never goes dead — the person who built it stays responsible to the end.
Are there monthly costs?＋
There may be a small hosting cost to keep the app running, and I tell you everything honestly before we sign. No hidden fees. That account is in your name, too (BYOC).
Can I use it even if I’m not good with computers?＋
Yes. If it’s hard to use, that means I built it wrong. Making it usable with just a few taps is my job.
My idea is still vague — can I still hire you?＋
Yes. Vague is normal. We sort it out together. “This part is a hassle” is enough to start — I’ll draw up the structure and the design.
Once we sign, what happens first?＋
It starts with me watching how you work, in person (or by video call). The order is: observe → design → build → hand over to you → ongoing support.
Customer and staff data is involved — is it safe?＋
All data is stored in an account under your name (BYOC). I don’t hold your information separately, and security is built into the design from the very start.
